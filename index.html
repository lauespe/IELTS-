Why IELTS?

IELTS has become one of the most popular English exams to certify people's level of English. The General Training version is usually required to obtain a work or residency visa, while the Academic version is a frequent requirement for those willing to do a degree abroad. Whether you are starting a new life in an English speaking country or you're eager to have an unforgettable experience, IELTS opens doors. Stop procrastinating!
